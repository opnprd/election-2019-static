--- title: UK General Election 2019 Results layout: page ---

This site contains the Results of the General Election of 12th December 2019.

There are two datasets:

  • Constituencies, holding summary details about the results per constituency, including votes cast, majority, electorate and percentage turnout (where captured). Significant stats from the previous General Election in 2017 are also included.
  • Candidates, presenting the results per candidate: votes, percentage share and percentage point change from General Election 2017.

In addition to the election night collection performed by Britain Elects and New Statesman the datasets are derived in part from the following sources

  • Electoral Commission
  • Parliament (2015 & 2017)
  • They Work For You (CC-BY-SA)
  • Democracy Club (CC-BY 4.0)
